ID:__demorpt-sales-by-productDESC:Here we select products with total sales to find out which is saled better.SQL:SELECT Products.ProductName AS "Product name", SUM(Orders.Freight) AS "Freight Sum" FROM ((`Order Details` AS `Order Details`     INNER JOIN Products AS Products ON (`Order Details`.ProductID  = Products.ProductID ))     INNER JOIN Orders AS Orders ON (`Order Details`.OrderID  = Orders.OrderID ))WHERE(  '{{LASTYEAR}}-01-01' <= Orders.OrderDate AND Orders.OrderDate < '{{THISYEAR}}-01-01' AND   Products.CategoryID in (2,3,4) )GROUP BY Products.ProductName
